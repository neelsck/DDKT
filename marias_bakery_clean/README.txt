MARIA'S BAKERY CONTRACT PACKAGE

This folder is intentionally plain. It contains only the Maria's Bakery
contract texts plus one context file for asking the business question.

Files:

contracts/C_LEASE_contract.txt
  Retail lease and loading addendum with rent, loading window, refrigerated
  vehicle consent, violation fee, cure period, and default language.

contracts/C_VAN_contract.txt
  Refrigerated vehicle purchase, financing, security, insurance, and
  placed-in-service language.

contracts/C_SUPPLIER_contract.txt
  Bakery ingredient supply agreement with monthly minimums, forecasts,
  cold-chain shortfall language, and a volume rebate.

contracts/C_WHOLESALE_contract.txt
  Wholesale breakfast delivery agreement with route start, monthly revenue,
  delivery deadline, late credits, at-risk status, cure, and termination.

context_question_expected_response.txt
  The business context, the question to ask, where the relevant facts appear
  in the contracts, and the expected response.

There are no JSON files in this package.
