RULES AND OBLIGATIONS
────────────────────────────────────────
  Rule R1: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: sell Products to Bakery
    Supplier shall sell to Bakery flour, butter, dairy products, yeast, eggs, fillings, paper packaging, refrigerated ingredients, and related bakery supplies (collectively, the 'Products').

  Rule R10: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: place weekly orders by Wednesday
    Bakery shall place weekly orders by Wednesday for Products needed the following week unless the parties agree on another ordering schedule.
    Trigger: After time period — Every Wednesday

  Rule R11: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: confirm accepted orders
    Supplier shall confirm accepted orders by email or portal notice.
    Trigger: Upon event — Supplier accepts an order from Bakery

  Rule R13: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: issue volume rebate credit
    If Bakery purchases at least $8,000.00 of Products in a calendar month, Supplier shall issue Bakery a $400.00 credit for that month.
    Trigger: When threshold crossed — Bakery purchases at least $8,000.00 of Products in a calendar month
      Duration: 1 calendar month
      Threshold: monthly_purchases gte 8000.00
    Effect: Payment — Issue $400.00 credit to Bakery
      Formula: 400.00
      From: Supplier → To: Bakery
      Cap: $400.00

  Rule R14: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: apply rebate as credit on next monthly statement
    Supplier shall apply the rebate as a credit on the next monthly statement unless the Agreement has ended.
    Trigger: When condition precedent holds — Agreement is ongoing
    Effect: Payment — Apply rebate as credit on next monthly statement
      Formula: rebate amount
      From: Supplier → To: Bakery
    Exception: R15

  Rule R15: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: pay rebate within thirty days after final reconciliation
    If the Agreement has ended, Supplier shall pay the rebate within thirty days after final reconciliation.
    Trigger: When condition precedent holds — Agreement has ended
      Duration: 30 days
    Effect: Payment — Pay rebate within thirty days after final reconciliation
      Formula: rebate amount
      From: Supplier → To: Bakery
    Constraint: Grace period
      Duration: 30 days
      Payment due within 30 days after final reconciliation

  Rule R16: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: count returned Products toward rebate threshold
    Returned Products do not count toward the rebate threshold.
    Constraint: Carve-out
      Returned Products excluded from rebate threshold calculation

  Rule R17: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: count credits toward rebate threshold
    Credits do not count toward the rebate threshold.
    Constraint: Carve-out
      Credits excluded from rebate threshold calculation

  Rule R18: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: count non-Product charges toward rebate threshold
    Non-Product charges do not count toward the rebate threshold.
    Constraint: Carve-out
      Non-Product charges excluded from rebate threshold calculation

  Rule R19: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: make refrigerated Products available for pickup within four-hour window
    Supplier shall make refrigerated Products available for pickup within a four-hour pickup window confirmed for the applicable order.
    Trigger: Upon event — Pickup window confirmed for applicable order
    Constraint: Controlling provision
      Duration: 4 hours
      Four-hour pickup window for refrigerated Products

  Rule R20: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: hold refrigerated Products in temperature-controlled conditions
    Refrigerated Products shall be held in commercially reasonable temperature-controlled conditions until pickup or delivery.

  Rule R22: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: cooperate in substitute sourcing upon Cold-Chain Shortfall
    Upon a Cold-Chain Shortfall, Supplier shall cooperate in substitute sourcing, expedited replacement, or reasonable order adjustment.
    Trigger: Upon event — Cold-Chain Shortfall occurs

  Rule R23: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: responsible for spoilage from cold-chain failure
    Supplier is responsible for spoilage caused by Supplier's failure to maintain required cold-chain conditions before pickup or delivery.
    Trigger: Upon event — Supplier fails to maintain required cold-chain conditions before pickup or delivery

  Rule R24: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: responsible for spoilage from Bakery delay
    Bakery is responsible for spoilage caused by Bakery delay, vehicle failure, failure to maintain refrigeration after pickup, or improper handling after risk of loss passes.
    Trigger: Upon event — Bakery delay, vehicle failure, failure to maintain refrigeration after pickup, or improper handling after risk of loss passes

  Rule R26: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: pay undisputed invoices according to standard payment terms
    Bakery shall pay undisputed invoices according to Supplier's standard payment terms.
    Trigger: Upon event — Invoice is undisputed and due according to Supplier's standard payment terms
    Effect: Payment — Pay undisputed invoice amount
      Formula: invoice amount
      From: Bakery → To: Supplier

  Rule R28: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: pay undisputed amounts when due
    Undisputed amounts remain payable when due.
    Trigger: On date — Undisputed amount due date arrives
    Effect: Payment — Pay undisputed amount
      Formula: undisputed amount
      From: Bakery → To: Supplier

  Rule R34: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: maintain confidentiality
    Pricing, forecasts, customer route volume, recipes, formulas, and business plans are confidential and may be used only to perform this Agreement.

  Rule R35: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: maintain confidentiality
    Pricing, forecasts, customer route volume, recipes, formulas, and business plans are confidential and may be used only to perform this Agreement.

  Rule R36: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: use confidential information for purposes other than performing Agreement
    Confidential information may be used only to perform this Agreement.

  Rule R37: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: use confidential information for purposes other than performing Agreement
    Confidential information may be used only to perform this Agreement.

  Rule R38: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: amend Agreement without writing
    This Agreement may be amended only in writing.

  Rule R39: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: amend Agreement without writing
    This Agreement may be amended only in writing.

  Rule R40: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: assign Agreement without consent
    Neither party may assign this Agreement without consent except in connection with a sale of substantially all assets or a merger.
    Exception: R42

  Rule R41: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Supplier → Bakery: assign Agreement without consent
    Neither party may assign this Agreement without consent except in connection with a sale of substantially all assets or a merger.
    Exception: R42

  Rule R5: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: purchase monthly minimum of Products
    While active, Bakery shall purchase not less than $4,500.00 of Products during each calendar month.
    Trigger: Upon event — Bakery signs a wholesale breakfast delivery contract requiring weekday deliveries
    Constraint: Minimum floor
      Value: $4,500.00
      Duration: 1 calendar month
      Monthly minimum purchase amount of $4,500.00

  Rule R9: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Supplier: provide rolling four-week forecast
    Bakery shall provide a rolling four-week forecast of expected Product needs while a wholesale delivery route is active.
    Trigger: When condition precedent holds — A wholesale delivery route is active
    Constraint: Controlling provision
      Duration: 4 weeks
      Rolling four-week forecast window

STATE TRANSITIONS
────────────────────────────────────────
  When in state "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations":

    On event "month end evaluation":
      Guard: monthly purchases  <  4500.00
      Month ends with purchases below minimum threshold
      Transitions to "Bakery failed to meet monthly minimum purchase requirement".

    On event "order placed":
      Bakery places weekly order by Wednesday
      Remains in "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "order confirmed":
      Supplier confirms accepted order
      Remains in "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "pickup completed":
      Bakery completes pickup within four-hour window
      Remains in "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "invoice payment":
      Bakery pays invoice on time according to standard payment terms
      → Payment: Bakery pays undisputed invoice per standard terms [invoice amount]
      Remains in "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "month end evaluation":
      Guard: monthly purchases  ≥  8000.00
      Bakery exceeds volume rebate threshold and earns credit
      → Payment: Issue $400 volume rebate credit for exceeding monthly threshold [400.00]
      Transitions to "Bakery exceeded volume rebate threshold for the month".

    On event "payment missed":
      Guard: days overdue  >  0
      Bakery fails to pay undisputed invoice by due date
      Transitions to "Bakery failed to pay undisputed invoice by due date".

    On event "cold chain shortfall":
      Supplier fails to maintain required temperature-controlled conditions
      → Forfeiture: Supplier assumes liability for spoilage due to cold-chain failure
      Transitions to "Supplier failed to maintain temperature-controlled conditions".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues termination notice
      → Cure window: 30-day notice period before termination becomes effective
      Transitions to "Party issued termination notice, waiting for notice period to elapse".

  When in state "Supplier failed to maintain temperature-controlled conditions":

    On event "cold chain restored":
      Cold-chain conditions restored and substitute sourcing resolved
      Transitions to "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues termination notice
      → Cure window: 30-day notice period before termination becomes effective
      Transitions to "Party issued termination notice, waiting for notice period to elapse".

  When in state "Agreement ended, performing final accounting and rebate settlement":

    On event "final settlement complete":
      All final obligations settled including rebate payment
      → Payment: Pay any remaining rebate balance within 30 days of final reconciliation [final rebate amount]
      Transitions to "Agreement completed with all obligations satisfied".

  When in state "Bakery failed to meet monthly minimum purchase requirement":

    On event "month end evaluation":
      Guard: monthly purchases  ≥  4500.00
      Bakery returns to compliance with monthly minimum
      Transitions to "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "month end evaluation":
      Guard: monthly purchases  ≥  8000.00
      Bakery exceeds volume rebate threshold and earns credit
      → Payment: Issue $400 volume rebate credit for exceeding monthly threshold [400.00]
      Transitions to "Bakery exceeded volume rebate threshold for the month".

    On event "payment missed":
      Guard: days overdue  >  0
      Bakery fails to pay undisputed invoice by due date
      Transitions to "Bakery failed to pay undisputed invoice by due date".

    On event "cold chain shortfall":
      Supplier fails to maintain required temperature-controlled conditions
      → Forfeiture: Supplier assumes liability for spoilage due to cold-chain failure
      Transitions to "Supplier failed to maintain temperature-controlled conditions".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues termination notice
      → Cure window: 30-day notice period before termination becomes effective
      Transitions to "Party issued termination notice, waiting for notice period to elapse".

  When in state "Bakery failed to pay undisputed invoice by due date":

    On event "payment received":
      Bakery pays overdue amount and returns to good standing
      → Payment: Bakery pays outstanding invoice amount [outstanding amount]
      Transitions to "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues termination notice
      → Cure window: 30-day notice period before termination becomes effective
      Transitions to "Party issued termination notice, waiting for notice period to elapse".

  When in state "Bakery exceeded volume rebate threshold for the month":

    On event "month end evaluation":
      Guard: monthly purchases  <  4500.00
      Month ends with purchases below minimum threshold
      Transitions to "Bakery failed to meet monthly minimum purchase requirement".

    On event "rebate applied":
      Rebate credit applied to monthly statement, returning to normal operations
      Transitions to "Normal operation with ongoing purchase and delivery obligations".

    On event "payment missed":
      Guard: days overdue  >  0
      Bakery fails to pay undisputed invoice by due date
      Transitions to "Bakery failed to pay undisputed invoice by due date".

    On event "cold chain shortfall":
      Supplier fails to maintain required temperature-controlled conditions
      → Forfeiture: Supplier assumes liability for spoilage due to cold-chain failure
      Transitions to "Supplier failed to maintain temperature-controlled conditions".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues termination notice
      → Cure window: 30-day notice period before termination becomes effective
      Transitions to "Party issued termination notice, waiting for notice period to elapse".

  When in state "Party issued termination notice, waiting for notice period to elapse":

    On event "notice period elapsed":
      Guard: days since notice  ≥  30
      Termination notice period completes, entering final reconciliation
      Transitions to "Agreement ended, performing final accounting and rebate settlement".
