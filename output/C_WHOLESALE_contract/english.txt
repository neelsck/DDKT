RULES AND OBLIGATIONS
────────────────────────────────────────
  Rule R1: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Supply agreed products
    Bakery shall supply the products, pack sizes, and daily order quantities agreed by the parties in writing.
    Trigger: Upon event — Products, pack sizes, and daily order quantities agreed by parties in writing

  Rule R10: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Invoice monthly
    Bakery shall invoice monthly.
    Trigger: After time period — End of each calendar month
    Effect: Rule activation — Invoice triggers payment obligation

  Rule R11: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Customer → Bakery: Pay undisputed invoice amounts
    Customer shall pay undisputed amounts within fifteen days after receipt of invoice.
    Trigger: Upon event — Receipt of invoice from Bakery
      Duration: 15 days
    Effect: Payment — Customer pays undisputed invoice amounts
      Formula: undisputed amount
      From: Customer → To: Bakery
    Constraint: Grace period
      Duration: 15 days
      Payment due within 15 days after receipt of invoice

  Rule R13: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Credit for each late delivery
    Bakery shall credit Customer $250.00 for each late delivery.
    Trigger: Upon event — Delivery occurs after 7:00 a.m. without written waiver
    Effect: Payment — Bakery credits Customer $250.00 per late delivery
      Formula: 250
      From: Bakery → To: Customer

  Rule R17: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Present and begin cure plan
    Bakery shall have ten days after at-risk notice to present and begin a commercially reasonable cure plan, including vehicle, driver, ingredient, or loading corrections as applicable.
    Trigger: Upon event — Receipt of at-risk notice from Customer
      Duration: 10 days
    Effect: Cure window — Bakery has 10-day window to present and begin cure plan
    Constraint: Grace period
      Duration: 10 days
      Ten-day cure period after at-risk notice

  Rule R20: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Customer → Bakery: Pay earned amounts upon termination
    Upon termination, Customer shall pay undisputed amounts earned before termination, less credits and offsets permitted by this Agreement.
    Trigger: Upon event — Agreement is terminated
    Effect: Payment — Customer pays undisputed amounts earned before termination minus credits and offsets
      Formula: earned amount - credits - offsets
      From: Customer → To: Bakery

  Rule R23: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Maintain commercial general liability insurance
    Bakery shall maintain commercial general liability insurance.
    Trigger: After time period — Continuous obligation throughout contract term

  Rule R24: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Maintain commercial auto liability insurance
    Bakery shall maintain commercial auto liability insurance.
    Trigger: After time period — Continuous obligation throughout contract term

  Rule R25: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Maintain cargo or spoilage coverage
    Bakery shall maintain any cargo or spoilage coverage reasonably appropriate for refrigerated food delivery.
    Trigger: After time period — Continuous obligation throughout contract term

  Rule R27: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Use confidential information only to perform Agreement
    Customer pricing, location volume, route information, and promotional plans are confidential. Bakery shall use such information only to perform this Agreement.
    Trigger: After time period — Continuous obligation throughout contract term and after termination

  Rule R28: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Use confidential information except for Agreement performance
    Bakery must not use confidential information except to perform this Agreement.
    Trigger: Upon event — Bakery receives or accesses confidential information

  Rule R3: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Make material substitutions without consent
    Bakery shall not make material substitutions without Customer's consent, except for emergency substitutions that are commercially reasonable and comparable in quality.
    Trigger: Upon event — Need for material substitution arises
    Exception: R4

  Rule R33: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Either Party → Other Party: Assign Agreement without consent
    Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's consent except in connection with a sale of substantially all assets or a merger.
    Trigger: Upon event — Party seeks to assign Agreement
    Exception: R34

  Rule R5: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Deliver breakfast orders by deadline
    Bakery shall deliver conforming breakfast orders to the applicable Customer location by 7:00 a.m. each weekday.
    Trigger: After time period — Each weekday during contract term
    Effect: Rule activation — If delivery is after 7:00 a.m. without waiver, delivery is late and triggers late delivery credit
    Constraint: Grace period
      Duration: until 7:00 a.m.
      Delivery must occur by 7:00 a.m. each weekday
    Exception: R6

  Rule R7: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Bakery → Customer: Maintain delivery logs
    Bakery shall maintain delivery logs showing departure time, arrival time, receiving location, and any shortage or rejection.
    Trigger: After time period — Continuous obligation for all deliveries

  Rule R9: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Customer → Bakery: Pay monthly route price
    Customer shall pay Bakery $18,000.00 per calendar month for the route if Bakery performs the month without termination.
    Trigger: When condition precedent holds — Bakery performs the month without termination
    Effect: Payment — Customer pays Bakery $18,000.00 per calendar month
      Formula: 18000
      From: Customer → To: Bakery

STATE TRANSITIONS
────────────────────────────────────────
  When in state "Bakery performing daily deliveries, all obligations current":

    On event "delivery late":
      Guard: late count  <  3
      Delivery after 7:00 a.m. without waiver triggers late delivery credit
      → Payment: Credit Customer $250 for late delivery [late delivery credit]
      Transitions to "One or more late deliveries recorded, credit applied but contract continues".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues 30-day termination notice
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "immediate termination":
      Guard: consecutive missed deliveries  ≥  3
      Three consecutive missed deliveries triggers immediate termination
      → Payment: Customer pays earned amounts minus credits and offsets upon termination [earned amount - credits - offsets]
      Transitions to "Agreement terminated, final settlement complete".

    On event "at risk threshold reached":
      Guard: late or missed count  ≥  3
      Three or more late/missed deliveries in rolling calendar month triggers at-risk status
      Transitions to "Three or more late/missed deliveries in rolling month, at-risk notice issued".

    On event "invoice issued":
      Bakery issues monthly invoice at end of calendar month
      Transitions to "Invoice issued, awaiting payment within 15-day term".

  When in state "Three or more late/missed deliveries in rolling month, at-risk notice issued":

    On event "delivery late":
      Guard: late count  <  3
      Delivery after 7:00 a.m. without waiver triggers late delivery credit
      → Payment: Credit Customer $250 for late delivery [late delivery credit]
      Transitions to "One or more late deliveries recorded, credit applied but contract continues".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues 30-day termination notice
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "immediate termination":
      Guard: consecutive missed deliveries  ≥  3
      Three consecutive missed deliveries triggers immediate termination
      → Payment: Customer pays earned amounts minus credits and offsets upon termination [earned amount - credits - offsets]
      Transitions to "Agreement terminated, final settlement complete".

    On event "at risk notice issued":
      Customer issues at-risk notice, opening 10-day cure window
      → Cure window: Bakery has 10 days to present and begin cure plan
      Transitions to "Bakery has 10 days to present and begin executing cure plan".

    On event "invoice issued":
      Bakery issues monthly invoice at end of calendar month
      Transitions to "Invoice issued, awaiting payment within 15-day term".

  When in state "Invoice issued, awaiting payment within 15-day term":

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues 30-day termination notice
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "payment received":
      Guard: days since invoice  ≤  15
      Customer pays invoice within 15-day term
      → Payment: Customer pays undisputed invoice amount within 15 days [undisputed amount]
      Transitions to "Bakery performing daily deliveries, all obligations current".

    On event "payment deadline exceeded":
      Guard: days since invoice  >  15
      Customer fails to pay within 15 days of invoice receipt
      Transitions to "Customer has not paid within 15 days of invoice".

  When in state "Bakery has 10 days to present and begin executing cure plan":

    On event "delivery late":
      Guard: late count  <  3
      Delivery after 7:00 a.m. without waiver triggers late delivery credit
      → Payment: Credit Customer $250 for late delivery [late delivery credit]
      Transitions to "One or more late deliveries recorded, credit applied but contract continues".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues 30-day termination notice
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "cure completed":
      Bakery successfully presents and begins cure plan, returns to active status
      Transitions to "Bakery performing daily deliveries, all obligations current".

    On event "cure failed":
      Bakery fails to cure within 10 days, Customer may terminate
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "invoice issued":
      Bakery issues monthly invoice at end of calendar month
      Transitions to "Invoice issued, awaiting payment within 15-day term".

  When in state "One or more late deliveries recorded, credit applied but contract continues":

    On event "delivery late":
      Guard: late count  <  3
      Delivery after 7:00 a.m. without waiver triggers late delivery credit
      → Payment: Credit Customer $250 for late delivery [late delivery credit]
      Transitions to "One or more late deliveries recorded, credit applied but contract continues".

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues 30-day termination notice
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "immediate termination":
      Guard: consecutive missed deliveries  ≥  3
      Three consecutive missed deliveries triggers immediate termination
      → Payment: Customer pays earned amounts minus credits and offsets upon termination [earned amount - credits - offsets]
      Transitions to "Agreement terminated, final settlement complete".

    On event "performance restored":
      Guard: late count  <  3
      Performance improves below at-risk threshold, returns to normal active state
      Transitions to "Bakery performing daily deliveries, all obligations current".

    On event "at risk threshold reached":
      Guard: late or missed count  ≥  3
      Three or more late/missed deliveries in rolling calendar month triggers at-risk status
      Transitions to "Three or more late/missed deliveries in rolling month, at-risk notice issued".

    On event "invoice issued":
      Bakery issues monthly invoice at end of calendar month
      Transitions to "Invoice issued, awaiting payment within 15-day term".

  When in state "Customer has not paid within 15 days of invoice":

    On event "termination notice issued":
      Either party issues 30-day termination notice
      Transitions to "Either party has given 30-day termination notice".

    On event "late payment received":
      Customer makes overdue payment
      → Payment: Customer pays overdue invoice amount [undisputed amount]
      Transitions to "Bakery performing daily deliveries, all obligations current".

  When in state "Either party has given 30-day termination notice":

    On event "termination effective":
      Guard: days since notice  ≥  30
      30-day notice period expires, final settlement executed
      → Payment: Customer pays earned amounts minus credits and offsets upon termination [earned amount - credits - offsets]
      Transitions to "Agreement terminated, final settlement complete".
