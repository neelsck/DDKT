RULES AND OBLIGATIONS
────────────────────────────────────────
  Rule R10: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Loading only during permitted window
    Unless Landlord gives prior written approval for a different window, Tenant's loading activity shall occur only between 6:00 a.m. and 8:00 a.m. on business days.
    Constraint: Controlling provision
      Duration: 6:00 a.m. to 8:00 a.m.
      Loading window restricted to 6:00 a.m. to 8:00 a.m. on business days
    Exception: R11

  Rule R12: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Block service lane or common areas
    Tenant shall not block the service lane, common areas, driveways, sidewalks, fire lanes, trash areas, or other tenants' access.
    Effect: Payment — Violation fee for obstruction
      Formula: 350.00
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord

  Rule R13: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Comply with loading requirements
    Tenant shall comply with all reasonable building rules, municipal requirements, insurance requirements, and safety directions applicable to loading activity.

  Rule R14: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Park/operate refrigerated vehicle without consent
    Tenant shall not park, idle, charge, connect to power, or operate a refrigerated vehicle at the Premises overnight without Landlord's prior written consent.
    Effect: Payment — Violation fee for unauthorized refrigerated vehicle
      Formula: 350.00
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord
    Exception: R15

  Rule R16: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Provide required information in consent request
    A request for refrigerated vehicle consent must identify the vehicle, license plate, refrigeration unit, expected hours of use, parking location, insurance coverage, cargo endorsement, noise rating, and any power requirements.
    Trigger: Upon event — Tenant seeks refrigerated vehicle consent

  Rule R19: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Pay violation fee for loading outside window
    If Tenant loads outside the permitted loading window, Tenant shall pay a violation fee of $350.00 per occurrence.
    Trigger: Upon event — Tenant loads outside permitted window without approval
    Effect: Payment — Violation fee per occurrence
      Formula: 350.00
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord
    Exception: R11

  Rule R2: Prohibition (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Convert premises to warehouse/commissary/distribution depot
    Tenant shall not convert the Premises into a warehouse, commissary, or distribution depot.
    Effect: Rule activation — Violation triggers default remedies

  Rule R20: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Pay violation fee for obstruction
    If Tenant obstructs common areas, Tenant shall pay a violation fee of $350.00 per occurrence.
    Trigger: Upon event — Tenant blocks service lane, common areas, driveways, sidewalks, fire lanes, trash areas, or other tenants' access
    Effect: Payment — Violation fee per occurrence
      Formula: 350.00
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord

  Rule R21: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Pay violation fee for unauthorized refrigerated vehicle
    If Tenant parks or operates a refrigerated vehicle without required consent, Tenant shall pay a violation fee of $350.00 per occurrence.
    Trigger: Upon event — Tenant parks, idles, charges, connects to power, or operates refrigerated vehicle without consent
    Effect: Payment — Violation fee per occurrence
      Formula: 350.00
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord
    Exception: R15

  Rule R23: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Cure unauthorized loading or parking violation
    Tenant shall have ten days after written notice to cure an unauthorized loading or parking violation. Cure may require removal of the vehicle, cessation of the unauthorized activity, provision of insurance evidence, or submission of a complete consent request.
    Trigger: Upon notice — Landlord provides written notice of violation
      Duration: 10 days
    Effect: Cure window — Tenant must cure violation within 10 days
    Constraint: Grace period
      Duration: 10 days
      Ten-day cure period after written notice

  Rule R25: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Maintain insurance for refrigerated vehicle
    Before using any refrigerated vehicle in connection with the Premises, Tenant shall maintain commercial auto liability coverage and cargo or spoilage coverage reasonably acceptable to Landlord.
    Trigger: When condition precedent holds — Before using refrigerated vehicle

  Rule R26: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Indemnify Landlord for loading/vehicle claims
    Tenant shall indemnify and hold Landlord harmless from claims, losses, fines, property damage, personal injury, or increased insurance costs arising from Tenant's loading operations or refrigerated vehicle activity, except to the extent caused by Landlord's gross negligence or willful misconduct.
    Trigger: Upon event — Claims, losses, fines, property damage, personal injury, or increased insurance costs arise from Tenant's loading or vehicle activity
    Effect: Payment — Indemnification payment to Landlord
      Formula: actual damages
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord
    Constraint: Carve-out
      Indemnity does not apply to extent caused by Landlord's gross negligence or willful misconduct

  Rule R3: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Ensure wholesale packing remains incidental
    Tenant must ensure wholesale packing remains incidental to bakery business and does not materially increase noise, odors, waste, traffic, utility use, or loading activity beyond retail bakery level.
    Constraint: Controlling provision
      Wholesale activity must remain incidental and not materially increase impacts

  Rule R4: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Obtain consent for change in use
    Any change in use requires Landlord's prior written consent.
    Trigger: Upon event — Tenant desires to change use of Premises

  Rule R6: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Pay monthly base rent
    Tenant shall continue to pay monthly base rent of $6,200.00 on the first day of each calendar month.
    Trigger: On date — First day of each calendar month
      Duration: 1 month
    Effect: Payment — Monthly base rent payment
      Formula: 6200.00
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord

  Rule R7: Obligation (inactive at formation)
    Tenant → Landlord: Pay additional rent charges
    Any charge, reimbursement, fee, or other amount payable by Tenant under this Addendum constitutes additional rent under the Lease.
    Trigger: Upon event — Any charge becomes payable under this Addendum
    Effect: Payment — Additional rent payment
      Formula: applicable charge amount
      From: Tenant → To: Landlord
